## Methodology

### Data Source

- **Database:** Cortellis Drug Intelligence (Clarivate Analytics)
- **Query Date:** 2025-12-20
- **Scope:** Comprehensive drug development data including phase, indications, and sponsorship

### Scoring System

Each drug candidate was scored based on development phase and IBD indication relevance:

| Phase | IBD-Specific | Non-IBD | Rationale |
|-------|--------------|---------|----------|
| FDA Approved / On Market | 7 | 4 | Highest validation; IBD-specific indicates direct relevance |
| Phase 3 Clinical | 3 | 2 | Late-stage validation; high probability of approval |
| Phase 2 Clinical | 2 | 1 | Mid-stage validation; proof-of-concept established |
| Phase 1 Clinical | 1 | 0.5 | Early validation; safety and PK/PD characterized |
| Preclinical | 0.1 | 0.1 | Minimal validation; target engagement shown |
| Discontinued / No Development | 0 | 0 | No active development |

**IBD-Specific Indications:** Inflammatory Bowel Disease, IBD, Crohn's Disease, Ulcerative Colitis

### Limitations

- JAK1 data could not be retrieved due to API timeout issues (extensive drug portfolio)
- Scoring reflects quantity and stage of development; does not assess quality or mechanism differentiation
- Historical/discontinued programs included in drug counts but scored at 0
- Repurposing potential for non-IBD drugs requires additional clinical validation
